Vane

— Animator/Concept Developer

Drawn in Adobe Photoshop, Programmed using Android Development Kit

April 2015

[scroll down for breakdown]

Collaborating with Pratik Prakash, we explored one of our first steps towards interactive animation. Vane is an interactive mobile animation that uses the gyroscope in a phone to determine a user's movement. The animation then corresposnds to the user's shaking of the phone. For this project, we wanted to give the user a sense of involvement in his/her experience.
