Orla's Hive

— Animator/Modeler

Created with Maya & Clay animation, Compiled in Adobe Premiere and After Effects

October 2014

[scroll down for breakdown]

Orla's Hive is a collaborative project that cleverly combines digital 3D animation with stop motion clay animation to create a surreal, fantasical story about a Queen Bee and her hive. Almost a third of our food depends on bee pollination; we heavily depend on them, but at the same time, bees repel us. In brief, the film describes the bizarre dependent relationship between humans and bees.

Process

beehive model

My role was to create and animate all the digital objects in the short film, as well as post production compilation. All digital modeling and animation was completed in Maya. I think the most nerve-racking step in the process was combining the clay animation with the digital aniamtion. We had to be very mindful that the lighting and color schemes corresponded correctly.I mostly looked at bee hives and honey bottle to design the bee hive. I wanted to reference space shuttles or air ships to give the hive an extraterrestrial appearance.
