Ama's Momento (Introduction)

[Ama's] Momento

Los Angeles Asian Pacific Film Festival
USC Pacific Asia Museum

Narrative Designer / Prototyper

“Ama” in Taiwanese translates to “Grandmother,” and Ama’s Momento is a museum for Yoko Kung (龔玉葉), a grandmother of our dear team lead. Ama's Momento is a VR installation that serves as a physical manifestation of her dreams. By sharing Ama’s stories, we are creating an intergenerational community through personal storytelling. On December 2017, the Momento team traveled to Taiwan. Together we embarked on this journey of self discovery to reconnect with our roots. Below is the first phase of our installation.


Maya, Unity, Oculus Rift, Adobe Suite, Photogrammetry, Projection Mapping
