Process

Project Rufus is a personal project inspired by the Disney movie Bolt. Rufus is an English terrier puppy with a sarcastic and lazy attitude but has a soft spot for tennis balls. I wanted to practice subtle facial expressions with his human-like eyebrows and exaggerated snout. Currently, Rufus is an "in progress" project and I am in the process of rigging and binding the skin onto the him.
