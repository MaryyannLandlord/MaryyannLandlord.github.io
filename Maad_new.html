Oculus NextGen Symposium

Experience Designer / Software Developer

MAAD is a mixed reality interactive installation that blends the physical and virtual environment through the use of physical textures, lights, and puzzles. Following the classic Hero's Journey archetype, the experience uses an Alien named Adam to play the role as a guardian/guide. Through Adam's help, the player solves various puzzles to restore the Power of Transformation back to Earth.
