Hi, I'm Maryyann.

I'm an Animator majoring in Electronic Time Based Art at Carnegie Mellon University. I love creating whimsical work that evokes memorable experiences and exploring ways to transform a viewer into a participant.

I've always believed in utilizing the power of technology to discover new forms of animation. Recently, I've been exploring interactive components that allow users to personalize his or her viewing experience.

When I'm not glued to the computer, I'm practicing piano or taking photos for the school newspaper. I adore horseback riding, Hayao Miyazaki movies, computer programming, and classical music. I have a strong interest and appreciation for graphic design, and recently picked up Guitar Hero 3. Currently, I am a digital artist intern at Disney Research, and provide assets ranging from 3d modeling, animation, and concept art for various research projects.
