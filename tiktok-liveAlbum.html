Case Study: Animal Album Effect

Background and Research

Problem:

The goal of livestreaming effects is to help build better engagement between hosts and audiences and increase the streaming duration. Current effects on LIVE are migrated from the For You Page (FYP), which focus on individual user content and aren’t designed for multi-host social environments.

User Research:

To get better context on our problem, we partnered with the PM team to understand user behavior on LIVE and any pain points with using migrated FYP effects

Design Solutions Breakdown

Thematic Album Effect

We hypothesized a thematic album effect would help increase livestreaming duration. An album is a collection of multiple effects that can be accessed through a single effect thumbnail. Having thematic roles under a single effect album gives hosts a sense of community when using the effect together. These characters share the same visual identity and design language.

Normal effects are not visually or physically connected to each

Album collects themed effects under one entrypoint

Feel a sense of inclusion to use the same collection of thematic effects

Full-body Interactions

  • Since 90% of livestream phones are stationary, we used full-body interactions to create a fun atmosphere and better bond hosts and audiences.
